Shivata-30: Art Exhibition Begins in Kathmandu

Marking the 8th anniversary of the Shivata Love Foundation, the art exhibition titled ‘Shivata 30’ officially opened on Tuesday at the Nepal Art Council, Babermahal. The exhibition was inaugurated by the foundation’s founder and chairperson, Rajani Upadhyay, who is also Nepal’s first female vice-chancellor of the Nepal Academy of Fine Arts.

Shivata Love Foundation Nepal continues social work for a better society.

The Shivata Love Foundation marked its 8th anniversary by opening the 'Shivata 30' art exhibition in Nepal. Founded in memory of Rajani Upadhyaya's late daughter, Shivata, the foundation aims to support education for underprivileged children and contribute to various social initiatives. The exhibition features works by established and emerging Nepali artists, honoring Shivata's legacy and inspiring youth.
